GENERAL FAQs

Why we need internet connection for using this library?

Yes,for some content display such as navigation bar and processes like sending mail,Internet connection is required so it is better to have Internet connection turned on for better experience.

Why we need internet connection for registration process?

Yes,for registering you need internet connection,since we will verify your email ID.

Why we need your Email ID on registration process?

On registration process,we will verify your email ID by sending you a mail about your login credentials and to verify your credentials.

Can we access this Library Management System online through any device?

No,"Library Management System" is created in such a way that it can be accessed only in computer/laptop which has contains entire "Library Management System" along with server and its components.

How many person can use this Library Management System simultaneously?

Currently only one person can login and can use this "Library Management System".

What will happen when second person try to
login when person is already Logged in?

If second person is successfully logged in ,then immediately first person using will be logged out without any notification.So better complete your work before asking others to login.

Who can use "Library Management System"?

All students and staffs who borrow books from library can use this "Library Management System".

Why should we use this "Library Management
System"?

Because it helps in various ways like books which you have taken,how much time you have for renewal,which are the books you have to return to library .

What is the main reason for the Library Management System?

The main reason is to help the Librarian and to reduce the workload of Librarian.It also helps with database management like which book was taken by Student/Staff.




LOGIN AND PROFILE RELATED FAQs

What are the ways to change your password?

You can change the your password in your profile section by entering your current password.


Please refer Forgot your password question if you don't know your current password.

Why we can't change Email ID in profile?

You cannot change Email ID in your profile section because we have authenticated your Email ID on your registration process so we can't allow you to change to new mail ID after registration.

Did you forget your password,need to reset the password?

Solution for your problem is to follow below steps carefully

1)You can request for your current password in forgot password page(which can be found in login page) by entering your registered Email ID.(Make sure you have internet connection turned "ON" before clicking send mail button).

2)On receiving mail about your password.You can login and change your password in your profile section.

How to change your Username and other details like Year and Section and Department?

You can the your Username and other details in profile page in near by textbox/dropdown list given next to the required field.




ENTRY,RENEWAL,BOOK RETURN RELATED FAQs

How many days is given for the book on Entry?

Initially 30 days are given for returning the book from Entry date.Books can be renewed for atmost 5 times.If the renewal is completed,then you have to return the book.

Where to find the return date of taken books?

You can find the return date of taken books in renewal page where you can select the book ID for viewing the return date.

How many times/days we can renew the book?

You can renew the book for atmost 5 times and also you can choose the number of days for which you want to renew the books.By default,time given is 30 days for a renewal,it can be reduced based on your request.

Can't renew the your books?

If you can't renew your books then it means that you have already used 5 renewals so kindly return the book to library.

Can't find the your book?

You can add the book in the table in book webpage by typing the book along with author.
